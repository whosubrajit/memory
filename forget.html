Ever Thought, Why Do We Forget?

  • 🧩 Encoding Failure

    Info never went in. Example: Forgetting someone’s name right after meeting.

  • ⏳ Storage Decay

    Info fades over time. Example: Forgetting math formulas years later.

  • 🔑 Retrieval Failure

    Can’t access stored info. Example: Tip-of-the-tongue moment.

  • ⚡ Interference

    Other info gets in the way of remembering.

    • 📘 Proactive

      Old password blocks new.

    • 📗 Retroactive

      New TikTok trend makes you forget old one.
